Detection Tips

📷 Photo quality matters — Use natural light and focus on affected area
🌿 Show clear symptoms — Include spots, lesions, or discoloration
🚫 Avoid blurry images — Hold phone steady or use tap-to-focus
📐 Close-up shots — Get within 20–30cm of the affected leaf or stem
🌡️ Early detection — Better results when disease is in early stages
