Storage Guidelines

⚠️

Storage Enemies

🌡️
Excess Heat
Speeds up respiration and accelerates spoilage. Keep below 25°C for most grains.
💧
Moisture
Primary cause of fungal growth and mycotoxins. Maintain grain moisture below 12-14%.
🐛
Insects & Pests
Weevils, beetles and rodents cause massive losses. Use approved pesticides and sealed storage.
🍄
Mold & Fungi
Grows in damp conditions. Causes aflatoxin contamination which is dangerous to health.
✅

Universal Best Practices

✓ Always clean and dry produce before storage
✓ Inspect storage facilities before use — repair cracks, holes
✓ Use raised wooden platforms, never store directly on floor
✓ Label bags/containers with crop name, date, and quantity
✓ Follow First-In-First-Out (FIFO) principle
✓ Keep storage area clean and free of debris
✓ Regularly monitor for temperature, moisture, and pests
✓ Use hermetic bags or metal silos for long-term storage
✓ Maintain records of storage date and expected quality life
